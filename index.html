Welcome to the Chicago Voice Actor's Meetup (of Chicago)!

My name is Pat, and I'm a professional Voice Actor living in Lincoln Park. I searched in vain to find a meetup of Voice Actors, so I decided to start my own. Want to work on scripts, voices, characters tones? Want to meet fellow working Voice Actors? Want to talk shop about the industry? Then this meetup is for you!

Who the hell am I?

Pat Beecher

As I mentioned up top, my name is Pat. I'm a 24 year old Northwestern Graduate living in Lincoln Park. By day, I'm a struggling actor, but by night I'm also a struggling actor. I'd like to meet with all you fellow struggling (or not struggling) actors so we can celebrate and commiserate. FAQ

Is this a joke?

No. This is not a joke. While I understand the levity of the page might be misleading, this is a real group and we would love for you to be a part of it.

Who is allowed to join?

Being a professional actor is exclusive enough. Anyone who is dedicated to becoming a professional Vocie Actor is allowed. All we ask is that you take it seriously (but not too seriously).

What do you do during these meetups?

We practice. We learn. We talk. We network. This is a chance to relax and get to know your fellow Voice Actors in a safe space.

Where are the meetups?

They currently take place at my apartment. Since I'm not at liberty to disclose my address on the world wide web, you can send me an email (patbeechervoice@gmail.com)for more information!

When and how long are the meetups?

The meetups are two hours long and take place on Saturdays at 4pm.

I can't make it at that time. What should I do?

Find a meetup that meets at a time you can attend.

What is the meaning of life?

I'm not your spiritual leader. Ask them.

Isn't it redundant to say Chicago twice in the title of the group?

No.
